## 1. Purpose

The parties wish to explore a potential business transaction involving the potential acquisition of ZenPost Studio (the "Transaction"). In connection with the Transaction, Seller may disclose certain confidential information to Buyer.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" includes:

a) **Business Information:**
   - Revenue, costs, profit margins
   - Customer lists and user data
   - Business plans and strategies
   - Marketing plans and materials

b) **Technical Information:**
   - Source code and algorithms
   - Architecture and design documents
   - Technical specifications
   - Trade secrets and know-how

c) **Financial Information:**
   - Financial statements
   - Projections and forecasts
   - Pricing information
   - Cost structures

d) **Product Information:**
   - Roadmaps and development plans
   - Feature specifications
   - User research and feedback

**Exclusions:** Information that:
- Is or becomes publicly available (not through Buyer's breach)
- Was known to Buyer before disclosure
- Is independently developed by Buyer
- Is rightfully received from a third party

---

## 3. Obligations of Receiving Party

Buyer agrees to:

a) **Maintain Confidentiality:**
   - Use the same degree of care as for own confidential information (minimum: reasonable care)
   - Not disclose to third parties without written consent
   - Only share with employees/advisors who need to know and are bound by confidentiality

b) **Limit Use:**
   - Use Confidential Information ONLY to evaluate the Transaction
   - Not use for any competitive purpose
   - Not reverse engineer or copy (except for evaluation)

c) **Protect Materials:**
   - Keep all documents secure
   - Not photograph, scan, or distribute
   - Return or destroy upon request

---

## 4. Term

This Agreement shall remain in effect for **2 years** from the date of signing.

Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for **3 years** after the agreement ends.

---

## 5. Return of Materials

Upon Seller's request or if Transaction is not pursued, Buyer shall:
- Return all Confidential Information (physical and electronic)
- Delete all copies from systems
- Certify in writing that materials are destroyed
- Within **10 business days** of request

---

## 6. No License or Rights

This Agreement does NOT grant:
- Any license to intellectual property
- Any right to use trademarks or brand
- Any ownership rights
- Any obligation to proceed with Transaction

---

## 7. Legal Disclosure

If Buyer is legally required to disclose (subpoena, court order):
- Provide Seller with prompt notice
- Allow Seller to seek protective order
- Disclose only minimum required information
- Cooperate with Seller's efforts to protect

---

## 8. No Warranty

Confidential Information is provided "AS IS" without warranty:
- Seller makes no guarantee of accuracy
- Seller may update or correct information
- Buyer conducts own due diligence

---

## 9. No Solicitation (Optional)

For **12 months** after disclosure, Buyer agrees NOT to:
- Solicit Seller's employees/contractors
- Interfere with Seller's business relationships
- Contact Seller's customers without permission

**Exception:** General job postings are allowed.

---

## 10. Remedies

Buyer acknowledges that:
- Breach may cause irreparable harm
- Monetary damages may be insufficient
- Seller is entitled to injunctive relief
- Seller may seek all available remedies

---

## 11. General Terms

**Governing Law:**
- This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [Jurisdiction]
- Disputes resolved in courts of [Jurisdiction]

**Entire Agreement:**
- This supersedes all prior agreements
- Modifications must be in writing

**Severability:**
- If any provision is invalid, others remain in effect

**Assignment:**
- Buyer may not assign without written consent
